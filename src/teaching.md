# Teaching

## Courses at Colorado School of Mines

### GPGN 318 — Applied Geophysics 1

[:fontawesome-brands-github: jcapriot/GPGN_318](https://github.com/jcapriot/GPGN_318){ .md-button }

Survey design, data acquisition, processing, and interpretation of positioning, gravity, electrical, and magnetic methods using field instrumentation. Open-source course materials including Jupyter notebooks, lab assignments, and data sets.

---

### GPGN 319 — Applied Geophysics 2

[:fontawesome-brands-github: jcapriot/gpgn-319](https://github.com/jcapriot/gpgn-319){ .md-button }

Continuation covering electromagnetic methods (time-domain and frequency-domain EM), with hands-on field instrumentation labs. Open-source materials covering forward modeling, data processing, and inversion workflows.

---

### GPGN 329 — Physics of the Earth 2

[:fontawesome-brands-github: jcapriot/GPGN-329](https://github.com/jcapriot/GPGN-329){ .md-button }

Foundational physics of time-varying fields: electromagnetic theory and elastic wave propagation and their interactions with earth materials. Jupyter-based assignments using SimPEG.

---

## Open Educational Resources

### Geophysics for Practicing Geoscientists (GPG)

[:fontawesome-brands-github: geoscixyz/gpg](https://github.com/geoscixyz/gpg){ .md-button } &nbsp; [geosci.xyz/gpg](https://gpg.geosci.xyz){ .md-button }

An open-source, interactive textbook covering the fundamentals of applied geophysics — DC resistivity, electromagnetics, gravity, magnetics, and seismic methods. Developed collaboratively through the GeoSci.xyz initiative at UBC. I have contributed content on electromagnetic methods, forward modeling, and inversion concepts.

---

## Tutorials & Workshops

### SKYTEM AEM Inversion — SEG Near Surface Webinar Series (2024)

[:fontawesome-brands-github: simpeg/segns-2024-tutorial](https://github.com/simpeg/segns-2024-tutorial){ .md-button }

A hands-on tutorial presented for the Society of Exploration Geophysicists Near Surface geophysics webinar series. Covers end-to-end airborne electromagnetic (AEM) data inversion using the SKYTEM system and SimPEG, from data loading and quality control through 1D inversion workflows.
